Constitution Card Matching Game

Rules for Constitution Card Matching Game

Context

  • Each card set has two types: Article Cards: Cards labeled with Articles of the Constitution (e.g., Article 17). Description Cards: Cards containing an image that represents the essence of the corresponding article (e.g., an image about untouchability for Article 17).
  • Game Board:The board consists of a total of 2 Ã— N cards (e.g., 10 cards for 5 pairs) randomly distributed across the grid.

Rules for Constitution Card Matching Game:

  • Objective: Match Article Cards (e.g., Article 17) with their Description Cards (e.g., image of untouchability).
  • Setup: Cards are shuffled and placed face-down in a grid.
  • Gameplay:
    1. Flip two cards per turn.
    2. If they match (Article + correct description), keep the pair and earn 10 points.
    3. If they don't match, flip them back and try again.
  • Winning: Match all pairs as quickly as possible to score the most points.
  • Twist: Unmatched cards may shuffle positions every 15 seconds.
