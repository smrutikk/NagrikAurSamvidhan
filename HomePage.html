About Us

Welcome to Nagrik Aur Samvidhan, a digital initiative designed to make the Constitution of India accessible and engaging for everyone. Our mission is to promote constitutional literacy, helping citizens understand their rights and duties in a simplified, interactive way. Through our gamified platform, we simplify complex legal language, making it easier for people of all ages and educational backgrounds to learn about the Preamble, Fundamental Rights, Directive Principles, and Fundamental Duties.

By offering various game formats, such as Spin a Wheel, Cards, and Board Games, we make learning about the Constitution fun and educational. Our team of educators, developers, and legal experts ensures the platform is inclusive, with multimedia elements and language translation features to reach diverse audiences across India.

At Nagrik Aur Samvidhan, we’re committed to empowering citizens to make informed decisions and actively participate in democratic governance. Explore our platform and play your part in building a stronger democracy!
